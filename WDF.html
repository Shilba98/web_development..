About

Growth strategy that
scales with you.

Brandentifoi is a fractional growth intelligence practice built for European seed-stage B2B founders who need senior marketing thinking without the cost of a full-time CMO.

Founded by Shilba Xavier — an M1 Digital Marketing & Data Analytics student at emlyon Business School Paris with 3+ years of B2B SaaS, ABM, and growth marketing experience across India and Europe — Brandentifoi operates at the intersection of brand strategy, go-to-market design, and commercial intelligence.

The methodology layers Discovery → Diagnostic → Strategy → Execution into a repeatable system that adapts to your stage, ICP, and runway.
